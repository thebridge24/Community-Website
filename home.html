We are not just Vivi and Jonas, but an ever-growing community of open, exciting people.

Since the beginning of 2022, we have been supporting you in making Braunschweig your home more quickly, whether you have moved here, moved back, or are simply curious.

Together with our cooperation partners, we show you the most beautiful places, coolest clubs & initiatives and create shared memories so that you can find connections more quickly.
